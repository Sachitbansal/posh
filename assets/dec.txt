“Is your organisation celebrating Women’s Day… or truly protecting women at workplace?”

Every year on International Women’s Day, organisations organise celebrations, appreciation posts and events.

But the real question is:

Do employees trust the POSH system in your organisation?

In many of my POSH awareness sessions, employees openly share that they hesitate to report harassment because they are unsure whether the process will be fair and confidential.

This is exactly why the POSH Act, 2013 emphasises:
• Awareness among employees
• Proper functioning of the Internal Committee (IC)
• Regular training and capacity building

A workplace becomes truly safe when employees trust the system meant to protect them.

This Women’s Day, organizations should move from symbolic celebration to meaningful compliance and cultural change.

Because a safe workplace is not created by policies alone.
It is created by awareness, accountability and leadership commitment.
