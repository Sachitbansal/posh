The Legal Mandate

Every Company with 10+ Employees
Must Comply. No Exceptions.

The POSH Act, 2013

The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 is a statutory requirement for all organisations employing 10 or more persons. Non-compliance is not an option — it is a legal obligation.

Mandatory under Indian Law

The Cost of Non-Compliance

Penalties up to ₹50,000 under Section 26. Cancellation of business licences. Reputational damage that no brand can afford. Many organisations are technically compliant on paper — but not audit-ready in practice. That gap is exactly where we help.

Up to ₹50,000 Penalty + Licence Cancellation

Are You Truly Compliant?

Paper compliance differs from practical compliance. Many organisations have POSH policies on record but lack a functioning Internal Committee, regular training, or an experienced external IC member — all of which are required by law. An audit would expose these gaps. We close them.
